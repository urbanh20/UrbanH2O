Urban H2O is an interactive map of water quality complaints in NYC by location.

In the aftermath of the Flint water crisis, we think it's important that people are aware of the water quality complaints being reported where they live. Since we started our project at Hack NY, we decided to start by pullling NYC Open Data.

Our web app enables you to see how water quality complaints are distributed in New York City and what types of water issues are being reported in your neighborhood.
